legal / privacy

privacy policy.

last updated: 16 june 2026

Endemic is built on the principle of digital autonomy. This policy explains exactly what we collect, why, and the control you retain over it. We keep this deliberately short, because we keep our data footprint deliberately small.

01collection

information we collect

We collect minimal data, and only what you choose to give us. When you reach out through a contact form on our site, we receive:

  • Your name or organization
  • Your email address
  • The message or scope details you submit

We do not require accounts, we do not build behavioural profiles, and we do not ask for information we have no reason to hold.

02usage

how we use your data

Submitted data is used solely for professional communication: responding to your enquiry, scoping potential work, and continuing the conversation you started. It is not used for marketing lists, resale, or any purpose unrelated to the reason you contacted us.

03third parties

third parties

We do not sell your data. Full stop.

To operate the site, we rely on a small set of standard third-party tools, for example, web analytics and other integrated services, purely for operational efficiency. Where these tools process data on our behalf, that data remains limited to what is described in this policy and is never shared for advertising or sale.

04security

data security

Security is not an afterthought; it is the premise. We prioritize digital autonomy and secure operational practices, minimizing where data lives and who can touch it. We retain submitted data only as long as it is needed for the communication it relates to, then dispose of it.

05your rights

your rights

The data is yours. You have the right to request access to the information we hold about you, or to request its deletion, at any time. To exercise either right, contact us using the contact form and we will action your request promptly.
